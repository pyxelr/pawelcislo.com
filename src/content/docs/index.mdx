**Paweł Cisło**, MSc – MLOps Engineer building and maintaining ML platforms for enterprise clients. Holds a Master of Science (Distinction) in Data Science and Computational Intelligence from Coventry University.

**Specializes in:**
- MLOps platform engineering (AWS, Kubernetes, Kubeflow, Argo CD, Docker, CI/CD, GitOps)

- End-to-end ML pipelines, from data acquisition to model deployment and monitoring

Dreams about revolutionizing education through digital transformation.
